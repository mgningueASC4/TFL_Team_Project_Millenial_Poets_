Samples

Untitled

i want to be so complete
i could light a whole city
and then
i want to have you
cause the two of
us combined
could set it
on fire.

-Rupi Kaur

Good Hair

Hey, Indian boy, why (why!) did you slice off your braids?
Do you grieve their loss? Have you thought twice about your braids?

With that long, black hair, you looked overtly Indian.
If vanity equals vice, then does vice equal braids?

-Sherman Alexie

from Book of Hours

The light here leaves you
lonely, fading

as does the dusk
that takes too long

to arrive. By morning
the mountain moving

a bit closer to the sun...

-Kevin Young
