The Millenial Poetry Workshop

The last Friday of every month, we come together for a "Poetry and Talk" night. The workshop starts at 4:00 pm and ends at 10pm. Millenial Poets invites various types of poetry organizations and collectives of all shapes and sizes to bring their unique formats and personalities to the worshop ground. The purpose of this workshop is to express people's thoughts, feelings, work, culture and diversity through poetry. Also to meet, teach, learn, listen, perform and collaborate with other people through the poetry field.

Social Advocacy
