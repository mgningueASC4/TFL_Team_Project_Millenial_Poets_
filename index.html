About Us

Millennial Poets define poetry and highlight the work of current millennials addressing various social issues that affect our communities at large. Issues that vary from local community issues to global issues. We want to help bring the community together and raise awareness on important problems. We want all types of people to be able to express their emotion and opinions through poetry a beautiful underappreciated art.
